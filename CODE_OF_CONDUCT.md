# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant]
unmodified. The full text of the standards, enforcement
responsibilities, scope, enforcement guidelines, and attribution
applies as published.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Reporting

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be sent to the project
maintainer at **ricardo.azambuja@gmail.com**. Reports are handled
privately. The maintainer will acknowledge receipt within 3
business days and follow the enforcement guidelines in the
Covenant.

For security vulnerabilities (not conduct issues), use the channel
described in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) instead.

## Translations

Translations of the Covenant are available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations>. The English
version at the link above is authoritative for this project.
